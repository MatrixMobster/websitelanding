  • Night Tabs

    Night Tabs (formerly TrueTabs) is an extensive CSS3 Tabbed Content snippet. It started out small as a typical CSS Tabs snippet, but I have expanded it greatly. It offers many features, and can be easily modified to fit your needs. Some of the features include: Cross browser support, preset animations, colors, and layouts, a responsive grid system, and a typography set, among other things.
